Stitch

Put your photos and videos in a row, export one video. That's the entire app.

No subscription. No account. No watermark. No data collected. $3.99, once.

Coming soon to the App Store

What it does

  • Pick photos and videos together — they line up in the order you pick them
  • Drag to reorder on a filmstrip timeline; preview exactly what you'll export
  • Cut like tape: split at the playhead, delete the piece you don't want, undo anything
  • Auto-cut: one tap fits everything into 10, 30, or 60 seconds — computed on your phone, not a server
  • Fit or Fill: whole frame with clean bars, or edge-to-edge
  • Export a portrait 1080×1920 MP4 straight to your photo library

What it will never do

No transitions, filters, stickers, text overlays, or effects packs. No "Pro" tier where those show up later. If you need them, you already know where to find them — Stitch is for when you don't.

Why $3.99, once

Because that's the entire business model. You pay once, you own it, and every feature works forever. There is nothing to unlock and no reason to nag you — the app can't even phone home, because it has no network access at all.

Updates are free, forever. Stitch keeps improving — shaped by feedback from the people who use it — and no improvement will ever cost you a second time or move behind a paywall.

Privacy policy

Stitch collects nothing. It has no network access — your photos and videos are processed on your device and never leave it. No analytics, no accounts, no identifiers, no third-party code. The App Store privacy label reads "Data Not Collected" because there is no code that could.

Effective date: 2026. This policy can only ever get shorter.
